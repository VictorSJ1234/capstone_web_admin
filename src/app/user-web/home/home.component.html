About the Mobile App

Mosquinator is a mobile application that aims to help users to determine different kinds of mosquito species and the diseases that it can cause. The application aims to help people to be aware of different mosquito borne-diseases and as well as to provide them a community based reporting platform that can be used for reporting related cases of mosquito diseases or an intel for maintaining cleanliness around area that could be possible breeding grounds of mosquitoes.
